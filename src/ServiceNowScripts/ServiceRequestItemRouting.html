Routing Options

  • Directly route to fulfillment group.
    1. Use the "Variables" section of this task to select the fulfillment group.
    2. Click the "Close Task" to continue. The status for the associated request will be automatically updated.
  • Route to group for approval.
    1. Use the "Variables" section of the "Route Service Request" task to select both the approval and the fulfillment groups.
    2. Click the "Close Task" button to continue. The status for the associated request will be automatically updated.
    3. Once (and if) the item is approved, the requested item will be re-assigned to the fulfillment groups.
  • Route to another group for assessment. If your group is not able to determine the appropriate routing option, you can simply assign the "Route Service Request" task to another group.
  • Cancel Request Item
    1. Change "State" field of the "Route Service Request" task to "Closed Skipped" or "Closed Incomplete".
    2. Click the "Update" button to save changes. The associated request item will be automatically updated.
